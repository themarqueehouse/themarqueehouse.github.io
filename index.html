What people say

Q: How early can I book the letters?

A: We recommend booking as early as possible to secure your dates, especially during peak event seasons.

Q: How long do the batteries last?

The batteries will comfortably last up to 18 hours. They will come with fresh batteries before any rental so no need to worry.

Q: Are the letters weather-resistant?

A: Our letters are designed for indoor and outdoor use. We don’t recommend exposure to heavy rain or extreme weather.
